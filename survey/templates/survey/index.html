Konza Data Centre Healthcare Management System (HMS) Questionnaire Survey

The Healthcare Management System (HMS) is a cross‐functional healthcare management platform that facilitates the effective and efficient management of a county’s entire healthcare system, managing and interconnecting all healthcare facilities from level 1 all the way to level 5, and further supporting CHWs/CHVs as well as Public Health management.

Our clients and facilities that use our platform do take pride in the fact that we have had significant impact in enhancing their care for their patients, markedly improved their financial performance, provided them with critical insights and controls at supply chain and provided them with research and in-depth analytics both historical and predictive to enable them improve patient care and their bottom line.

We highlight the impact we have had in four critical areas, enumerated below:

Financial click to view

Across all our clients, we have seen a 38% increase in financial performance upon the installation of the system, with a 18% increase in facilities that had a system before and a 60% increase in facilities that were moving from a paper/manual based operation.

We have not seen any decline in revenue from any client who has setup our platform. Most clients attribute this to the controls provided by the Collabmed platform.

Clinical care click to view

This is the crux of healthcare and this is where we have had the greatest impact. The goal of automation is to significantly improve patient care, at individual patient level and as well as enhancing patient care in general by relying on insights generated from patient care at the facility.

Patient throughput click to view

We have seen a significant increase in patient traffic, brought about by the efficiencies of the Collabmed platform. An increase in 24% patients seen throughput on a daily basis is the median our clients experience

Patient engagement click to view

A reduction in attrition rates for appointments and medications is another positive factor we have analysed with our clients. Through the integrated patient communication tools and analysis, we have a seen a 40% drop in attrition rates for appointments.
A key example of a client we have worked with, saw a marked increase of 56% in MCH visits once we set up our platform and follow on ANC visits.

Clinical treatment click to view

Through peer review mechanisms that our platform supports, clinicians have been able to walk through their treatment choices as a collective and improve on the same. We have seen a reduction in mis-diagnosis as well as a reduction in erroneous lab and imaging results.

Supply chain click to view

A 90% reduction in stock outs and an equally significant reduction of 85% in purchase of short expiry drugs is a major plus of our platform. The ability to have a global view of their inventory has also enabled multi-location facilities to move around their stock to those facilities that require it.
